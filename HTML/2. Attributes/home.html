About GIVA

  • About GIVA(jee-va)
GIVA is a fine jewellery brand of Silver, Gold, and Lab-Grown Diamonds, headquartered in Bengaluru. We have grown to become the largest D2C jewellery brand in India, earning people's trust as the go-to choice for gifting. GIVA's journey shines even brighter! GIVA strives to bridge the gap between the ease of online shopping and the delight of in-store experiences with its omnichannel approach. Over the last five years, GIVA has partnered with various celebrities like Neha Kakkar, Shruti Haasan, Diana Penty, and& Bhumi Pednekar to promote exclusive collections. Renowned actor Anushka Sharma is our brand ambassador.

  • What does the name GIVA mean?
The GIVA rhombus captures the magic of life's tiny sparkles: a twinkling eye, a warm smile, a fleeting spark of joy. The logo at the end of our name is also a nod to the inspiration behind choosing the word GIVA. Derived from the word 'hill' in Sanskrit, our logo reflects our name's meaning and is a potent symbol of your unstoppable climb. It is also inspired by the Sanskrit word "jeeva" (जीव), meaning life, symbolising our aspiration to celebrate the beauty of life through our jewellery creations.

Meet our Founders

NIKITA PRASAD

Nikita Prasad, Co-founder and Creative Head at GIVA (Silver. Gold. Lab Grown Diamonds), brings a wealth of expertise to the table. With a background in fashion from the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) and a solid foundation in the jewellery industry, Nikita launched her career as an intern in a jewellery design department. Through the years she has honed her meticulous attention to detail, a quality that has been a driving force behind GIVA.
